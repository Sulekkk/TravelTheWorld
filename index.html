Pack your bags. We’re going on vacation!

That’s exactly what an effective travel website should make you feel. It should activate the travel bug with vibrant imagery and vivid copy. And while photos can do a lot of the work in inspiring visitors, a travel site can’t accomplish its purpose without great web design.

Web design plays a major role in visitors’ first impressions.

In fact, in one study, when participants were asked why they distrusted a website, 94% of the comments were about design. For a travel site, this means that design can be the determining factor in whether a visitor trusts recommendations and information.

Why you should travel on your own?

There are several reasons why travelling on your own can make your future journeys unforgettable and more efficient. Let us show you just few examples.

The price

A lot of people are not aware that travelling on your own can be much cheaper. The most important thing is that you don't have to pay travel agency their commision. It only costs your time to organise a trip but in our opinion it is really worth it!

Getting to know

When you are organizer of your trip you have unique chance to get know the culture and people. It is one of our favourite advantages. Nothing give you a better chance to feel the place than trying to be part of it.

No limits

Do you like feeling free and independent? If yes, this type of travelling is made for you. No time limits, no schedules and no rush. Just go and do whatever you want and enjoy every moment as you really like.
